About Me

Hello! My name is Orhan Gumus, and I am a Mechanical Engineering student with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence and its applications in healthcare. I am particularly interested in how AI can improve patient care, optimize medical systems, and support clinical decision-making.

This portfolio showcases my work throughout the AI in Healthcare course. It reflects my learning journey, hands-on projects, and understanding of how artificial intelligence can be applied to real-world healthcare challenges.

Course Overview

In this course, I explored key concepts related to Artificial Intelligence in healthcare, including:

Machine learning applications in medicine
Natural Language Processing (NLP) in healthcare
Reinforcement Learning in treatment optimization
Human-Centered AI Design (HCD)
Ethical considerations such as bias, fairness, and privacy

Each lab and assignment helped me understand how AI systems can be designed responsibly and effectively in healthcare environments.
